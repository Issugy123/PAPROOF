Bubble Cosmetics Partners with Local Artisans

We're thrilled to announce our new initiative supporting Filipino artisans through sustainable packaging collaborations and community development programs. At Bubble Cosmetics, we've always believed that beauty should not only enhance personal confidence but also contribute positively to our communities and environment.

The Artisan Collaboration Initiative

Our Artisan Collaboration Initiative aims to celebrate Filipino craftsmanship while creating sustainable packaging solutions that reduce our environmental footprint. We've partnered with artisan communities across the Philippines to create handcrafted packaging elements for our products, providing economic opportunities while preserving traditional crafts.

Artisans from Bicol creating handwoven packaging elements from abaca fiber

Our Artisan Partners

We're working with several artisan communities across the Philippines, each bringing their unique traditional crafts to our packaging:

  • Abaca Weavers of Bicol - Creating handwoven pouches and wraps from sustainable abaca fiber
  • Bamboo Craftsmen of Iloilo - Producing bamboo containers for our limited-edition gift sets
  • Indigenous Textile Artists of Mindanao - Designing fabric wraps using traditional patterns and natural dyes
  • Recycled Paper Artisans of Rizal - Crafting boxes and paper elements from recycled materials

Each artisan group works with our design team to create packaging elements that honor their traditional techniques while meeting our product needs and sustainability standards.

Sustainable Packaging Solutions

The collaboration has already yielded several innovative packaging solutions that will be rolling out across our product lines:

Our new limited-edition gift sets featuring handcrafted bamboo containers

  1. Abaca Fiber Gift Wraps - Replacing plastic bubble wrap with handwoven abaca fiber wraps that are both protective and beautiful
  2. Bamboo Product Containers - Limited-edition bamboo containers for our bestselling products, designed to be reused as storage or decor
  3. Handwoven Cosmetic Pouches - Complimentary pouches made from indigenous textiles included with purchases over ₱2,000
  4. Recycled Paper Packaging - All paper packaging elements now made from 100% post-consumer recycled materials, hand-finished by local artisans

These packaging innovations not only reduce waste but create unique unboxing experiences that showcase Filipino craftsmanship.

"Working with Bubble Cosmetics has allowed us to preserve our traditional weaving techniques while creating sustainable income for our community. It's wonderful to see our craft reaching new audiences and being appreciated for both its beauty and functionality."

— Maria Luisa Santos, Lead Weaver, Abaca Weavers of Bicol

Community Development Programs

Beyond creating sustainable packaging, our partnership includes several community development initiatives:

A skills development workshop for young artisans in Iloilo

  • Skills Training Programs - Workshops for young community members to learn traditional crafts from master artisans
  • Business Development Support - Training in business management, pricing, and marketing to help artisan groups develop sustainable business models
  • Educational Scholarships - Funding for children from artisan communities to pursue education in design, business, or environmental science
  • Community Infrastructure - Investment in workspace improvements, tools, and sustainable material sourcing

These programs ensure that our partnerships create lasting positive impact beyond the immediate economic benefits of our product collaborations.

Environmental Impact

The environmental benefits of this initiative are significant:

  • Reduction in plastic packaging by 75% across our product lines
  • Use of renewable, biodegradable materials that can be composted at end-of-life
  • Support for sustainable harvesting practices that protect local ecosystems
  • Lower carbon footprint through local sourcing and production

We're committed to measuring and reporting on these environmental impacts as part of our annual sustainability report.

Looking Forward

This initiative is just the beginning of our commitment to sustainable, community-centered business practices. In the coming year, we plan to:

  • Expand our artisan partnerships to include more communities across the Philippines
  • Develop a limited-edition product line featuring designs inspired by Filipino cultural heritage
  • Launch a "Meet the Makers" campaign to highlight the stories and skills of our artisan partners
  • Host workshops where customers can learn traditional crafts directly from our artisan partners

We believe that beauty products should not only make you look and feel good but also do good for communities and the planet. Our partnership with local artisans is a key step in living this value.

1. Introduction

At Bubble Cosmetics, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

2. The Data We Collect About You

Personal data means any information about an individual from which that person can be identified. We may collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of personal data about you which we have grouped together as follows:

  • Identity Data includes first name, last name, username or similar identifier.
  • Contact Data includes billing address, delivery address, email address and telephone numbers.
  • Financial Data includes payment card details.
  • Transaction Data includes details about payments to and from you and other details of products you have purchased from us.
  • Technical Data includes internet protocol (IP) address, your login data, browser type and version.
  • Profile Data includes your username and password, purchases or orders made by you, your preferences.
  • Usage Data includes information about how you use our website and products.
  • Marketing and Communications Data includes your preferences in receiving marketing from us.

3. How We Use Your Personal Data

We will only use your personal data when the law allows us to. Most commonly, we will use your personal data in the following circumstances:

  • Where we need to perform the contract we are about to enter into or have entered into with you.
  • Where it is necessary for our legitimate interests and your interests and fundamental rights do not override those interests.
  • Where we need to comply with a legal or regulatory obligation.

4. Data Security

We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used or accessed in an unauthorized way, altered or disclosed. In addition, we limit access to your personal data to those employees, agents, contractors and other third parties who have a business need to know.

5. Your Legal Rights

Under certain circumstances, you have rights under data protection laws in relation to your personal data, including:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction of your personal data.
  • Request erasure of your personal data.
  • Object to processing of your personal data.
  • Request restriction of processing your personal data.
  • Request transfer of your personal data.
  • Right to withdraw consent.
