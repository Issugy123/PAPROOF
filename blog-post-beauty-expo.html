Join Us at the Manila Beauty Expo 2025

We're excited to announce that Bubble Cosmetics will be participating in the Manila Beauty Expo 2025, the Philippines' largest and most prestigious beauty event of the year! Mark your calendars for June 12-14, 2025, at the SMX Convention Center Manila, where we'll be showcasing our latest products, offering exclusive experiences, and connecting with our wonderful community.

Our Biggest Booth Yet

This year, we're going all out with our largest and most interactive booth ever! Located at Booth #A12-15 in the main exhibition hall, our 120-square-meter space has been designed as an immersive "Bubble Beauty Playground" with multiple zones for you to explore:

  • The Bubble Bar - Try our complete product range with guidance from our beauty experts
  • The Glow Studio - Get complimentary mini-makeovers featuring our new Bubble Glow skincare line
  • The Digital Beauty Zone - Experience our AR makeup try-on technology and create shareable beauty content
  • The Sustainability Corner - Learn about our partnerships with local artisans and our eco-friendly initiatives
  • The Shop & Save Area - Enjoy exclusive expo-only discounts and limited-edition product bundles

Exclusive Product Launches

The Manila Beauty Expo will be the first place to experience several exciting new Bubble Cosmetics products:

Sneak peek of our new Bubble Pop Lip & Cheek Tints

  1. Bubble Pop Lip & Cheek Tints - A collection of 8 vibrant, buildable tints in adorable bubble-shaped packaging
  2. Bubble Glow Body Care Line - Expanding our popular skincare line to include body products
  3. Limited Edition Manila Collection - A makeup collection featuring colors inspired by Manila's vibrant culture and landscapes

Expo attendees will have the opportunity to purchase these products before they're available in stores or online!

Meet Our Beauty Experts

Throughout the three-day event, our booth will host a series of mini-masterclasses and demonstrations by top beauty professionals:

Friday, June 12

  • 11:00 AM - 12:00 PM: "Skincare Basics for Filipino Skin" with Dr. Ana Reyes, Dermatologist
  • 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM: "5-Minute Everyday Makeup" with Sofia Mendoza, Makeup Artist
  • 5:00 PM - 6:00 PM: "Sustainable Beauty Practices" with Carlos Tan, Sustainability Director

Saturday, June 13

  • 11:00 AM - 12:00 PM: "Color Theory for Morena Skin" with Sofia Mendoza, Makeup Artist
  • 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM: "Humidity-Proof Makeup Tips" with Mia Lim, Beauty Influencer
  • 5:00 PM - 6:00 PM: "Meet & Greet with Heart Evangelista" (Bubble Cosmetics Brand Ambassador)

Sunday, June 14

  • 11:00 AM - 12:00 PM: "Bubble Glow Skincare Routine" with Dr. Ana Reyes, Dermatologist
  • 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM: "Creative Makeup Looks" with Paolo Santos, Celebrity Makeup Artist
  • 5:00 PM - 6:00 PM: "Closing Party & Raffle Draw" with the entire Bubble Cosmetics team

Seating for these sessions is limited, so we recommend arriving early to secure your spot!

Exclusive Expo Offers

We've prepared some amazing deals and gifts exclusively for Manila Beauty Expo attendees:

  • 20% off all regular-priced items purchased at our booth
  • Free Bubble Beauty Bag with purchases over ₱2,000 (valued at ₱1,200)
  • Limited-edition Manila Collection Bundle at 30% off regular pricing
  • Daily raffle for a chance to win a year's supply of Bubble Cosmetics products
  • Exclusive discount codes for future online purchases

"The Manila Beauty Expo is always a highlight of our year! It's a wonderful opportunity to connect directly with our community, showcase our innovations, and celebrate Filipino beauty. We can't wait to see everyone there!"

— Emma Chen, Founder & CEO

Getting Your Tickets

The Manila Beauty Expo 2025 is expected to sell out quickly. Here's how you can secure your tickets:

  1. General Admission: ₱350 per day or ₱800 for a 3-day pass
  2. VIP Pass: ₱1,500 (includes priority access to all masterclasses, VIP lounge access, and an exclusive goodie bag)

As a special treat for our loyal customers, we're giving away 50 FREE General Admission tickets! To enter the giveaway:

  1. Follow @bubblecosmeticsph on Instagram
  2. Share our Beauty Expo announcement post
  3. Tag 3 friends and use the hashtag #BubbleAtBeautyExpo2025

Winners will be announced on our social media channels on May 15, 2025.

1. Introduction

At Bubble Cosmetics, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

2. The Data We Collect About You

Personal data means any information about an individual from which that person can be identified. We may collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of personal data about you which we have grouped together as follows:

  • Identity Data includes first name, last name, username or similar identifier.
  • Contact Data includes billing address, delivery address, email address and telephone numbers.
  • Financial Data includes payment card details.
  • Transaction Data includes details about payments to and from you and other details of products you have purchased from us.
  • Technical Data includes internet protocol (IP) address, your login data, browser type and version.
  • Profile Data includes your username and password, purchases or orders made by you, your preferences.
  • Usage Data includes information about how you use our website and products.
  • Marketing and Communications Data includes your preferences in receiving marketing from us.

3. How We Use Your Personal Data

We will only use your personal data when the law allows us to. Most commonly, we will use your personal data in the following circumstances:

  • Where we need to perform the contract we are about to enter into or have entered into with you.
  • Where it is necessary for our legitimate interests and your interests and fundamental rights do not override those interests.
  • Where we need to comply with a legal or regulatory obligation.

4. Data Security

We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used or accessed in an unauthorized way, altered or disclosed. In addition, we limit access to your personal data to those employees, agents, contractors and other third parties who have a business need to know.

5. Your Legal Rights

Under certain circumstances, you have rights under data protection laws in relation to your personal data, including:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction of your personal data.
  • Request erasure of your personal data.
  • Object to processing of your personal data.
  • Request restriction of processing your personal data.
  • Request transfer of your personal data.
  • Right to withdraw consent.
