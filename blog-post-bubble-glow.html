Introducing Bubble Glow: Our New Skincare Line

We are thrilled to announce the launch of Bubble Glow, our first-ever complete skincare line specifically formulated for Filipino skin! After two years of intensive research and development in collaboration with leading dermatologists in the Philippines, we've created a collection that addresses the unique skincare needs of Southeast Asian skin in our tropical climate.

Skincare Designed for Filipino Skin

Filipino skin has distinct characteristics that require specialized care. Our research showed that many international skincare brands don't adequately address concerns like:

  • Higher oil production due to humidity
  • Increased melanin production and hyperpigmentation concerns
  • Sensitivity to certain ingredients common in Western formulations
  • Unique needs of combination skin in tropical climates

Bubble Glow was developed to fill this gap, providing effective skincare solutions that work harmoniously with Filipino skin biology and our environmental conditions.

The complete Bubble Glow skincare collection

The Bubble Glow Collection

Our new skincare line includes seven essential products, each formulated with our proprietary Glow Complex™ – a blend of indigenous Filipino botanical extracts with proven skincare benefits:

1. Gentle Cleansing Gel (₱650)

A pH-balanced cleanser that effectively removes impurities without stripping the skin's natural moisture. Enriched with calamansi extract and niacinamide to brighten while cleansing.

2. Balancing Toner (₱750)

A hydrating, alcohol-free toner with salicylic acid and guava leaf extract to refine pores and control excess oil while maintaining optimal hydration levels.

3. Vitamin C Brightening Serum (₱1,200)

A powerful 10% stabilized vitamin C serum enhanced with kakadu plum and calamansi extracts to fade dark spots, even skin tone, and protect against environmental damage.

4. Hydra-Boost Essence (₱950)

A lightweight essence with five types of hyaluronic acid and coconut water to deeply hydrate without heaviness – perfect for our humid climate.

Our lightweight formulas absorb quickly without feeling heavy or greasy

5. Oil-Control Moisturizer SPF 50 (₱850)

A dual-action moisturizer with broad-spectrum sun protection, bamboo extract to control shine, and niacinamide to strengthen the skin barrier.

6. Overnight Repair Mask (₱980)

A restorative sleeping mask with centella asiatica, bakuchiol (a gentle retinol alternative), and banana flower extract to repair and rejuvenate skin overnight.

7. Spot Treatment Gel (₱580)

A targeted treatment with tea tree oil, salicylic acid, and narra tree extract to quickly reduce blemishes without causing dryness or irritation.

Sustainable and Ethical Formulations

In line with our commitment to sustainability, the Bubble Glow line features:

  • Ethically sourced ingredients, with priority given to local Filipino botanicals
  • Recyclable packaging made from 70% post-consumer recycled materials
  • Refill options for all products to reduce packaging waste
  • Cruelty-free formulations with no animal testing
  • Free from harmful ingredients like parabens, sulfates, and artificial fragrances

We've also partnered with local communities in the Philippines to sustainably source our botanical ingredients, providing fair wages and supporting traditional farming practices.

Clinical Results

Our Bubble Glow line underwent extensive testing with 200 Filipino participants across different regions of the Philippines. After 8 weeks of use:

  • 92% reported visibly brighter, more even-toned skin
  • 89% saw a reduction in oiliness and shine
  • 94% experienced fewer breakouts and blemishes
  • 97% found the products comfortable to use in hot, humid conditions

"Developing Bubble Glow has been a labor of love and scientific innovation. We wanted to create products that truly understand and address the unique needs of Filipino skin, using the incredible botanical resources our country has to offer. The result is a skincare line that delivers visible results while honoring our commitment to sustainability and ethical practices."

— Dr. Maricel Santos, Head of Product Development

  • Brightening Duo (Vitamin C Serum and Overnight Mask): ₱1,950 (save ₱230)

The first 500 customers to purchase any Bubble Glow product will receive a limited-edition skincare organizer and samples of our upcoming body care line.

We can't wait for you to experience the Bubble Glow difference – skincare that truly understands Filipino skin!

1. Introduction

At Bubble Cosmetics, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

2. The Data We Collect About You

Personal data means any information about an individual from which that person can be identified. We may collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of personal data about you which we have grouped together as follows:

  • Identity Data includes first name, last name, username or similar identifier.
  • Contact Data includes billing address, delivery address, email address and telephone numbers.
  • Financial Data includes payment card details.
  • Transaction Data includes details about payments to and from you and other details of products you have purchased from us.
  • Technical Data includes internet protocol (IP) address, your login data, browser type and version.
  • Profile Data includes your username and password, purchases or orders made by you, your preferences.
  • Usage Data includes information about how you use our website and products.
  • Marketing and Communications Data includes your preferences in receiving marketing from us.

3. How We Use Your Personal Data

We will only use your personal data when the law allows us to. Most commonly, we will use your personal data in the following circumstances:

  • Where we need to perform the contract we are about to enter into or have entered into with you.
  • Where it is necessary for our legitimate interests and your interests and fundamental rights do not override those interests.
  • Where we need to comply with a legal or regulatory obligation.

4. Data Security

We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used or accessed in an unauthorized way, altered or disclosed. In addition, we limit access to your personal data to those employees, agents, contractors and other third parties who have a business need to know.

5. Your Legal Rights

Under certain circumstances, you have rights under data protection laws in relation to your personal data, including:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction of your personal data.
  • Request erasure of your personal data.
  • Object to processing of your personal data.
  • Request restriction of processing your personal data.
  • Request transfer of your personal data.
  • Right to withdraw consent.
